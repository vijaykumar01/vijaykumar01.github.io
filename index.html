Vijay Kumar

I am a senior data scientist at Walmart Labs Bangalore working on recommendation systems for online e-commerce applications. Previously, I was a post-doctoral researcher in the Willow team at INRIA Paris where I worked with Ivan Laptev. I obtained my PhD at IIIT Hyderabad under the guidance of Prof. C V Jawahar and Prof. Anoop Namboodiri.

I am interested in computer vision and machine learning specifically on human understanding in images and videos.
